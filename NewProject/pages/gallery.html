BASED MIXED MARTIAL ARTS

Wrestling

Wrestling is one of the strongest foundations in Mixed Martial Arts. Having good wrestling gives the ability to dictate where the fight takes place.

Grappling/Submission

Grappling is what led to the birth of Mixed Martial Arts. Control opponents with grappling which can lead to Submissions.

Striking

Striking is still one of the growing aspects of Mixed Martial Arts. There are many styles of striking such as Boxing, Karate, Taekwondo, Kickboxing & Many More.
