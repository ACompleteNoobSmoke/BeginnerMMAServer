Boxing

Boxing is still one of the growing aspects of Mixed Martial Arts. Boxers have good,crisp hand combinations, head & foot movement that can result in devasting knockouts

Kickboxing

Kickboxers use a combination of punching and kicking to keep their opponent broken down and guessing where strikes will come from.

Muay Thai

Muay Thai is The Art Of Eight Limbs. It is a striking style that the fighter utilizes Hands, Legs, Elbows & Knees to Brutalize the opponent.

Brazilian Jiu Jitsu

Brazilian Jiu Jitsu is what led to the birth of Mixed Martial Arts & UFC. Control opponents with grappling which can lead to Submissions.

Judo

Judo is still one of the growing aspects of Mixed Martial Arts. This grappling style is using your opponents momentum against them leading to impactful Throws & Submissions.

Wrestling

Wrestling is one of the strongest foundations in Mixed Martial Arts. Having good wrestling gives the ability to dictate where the fight takes place.
